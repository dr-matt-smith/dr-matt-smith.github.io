Direct Manipulation
(Shneiderman 1982)
lVisibility of objects of interest
lIncremental action at the interface
–rapid / real-time feedback on all actions
lReversibility of all actions
–Little/no penalty for user exploration
lSyntactic correctness of all actions
–So all user actions are legal
lCommunication language of ACTIONS to manipulate objects DIRECTLY
–As opposed to command-based languages
